Tychonoff separation axioms

There are various restrictions we make on the kinds of topological space we wish to consider. We call the classic restrictions Tychonoff separation axioms, following a Soviet Russian mathematician Andrey Tychonoff.

We abbreviate the axioms with the letter “T” after their German translation: Trennungsaxiom.
